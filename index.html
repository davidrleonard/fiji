Water is a human right.

Help turn it back on.

Detroit Water Project matches 8,000+ donors across the nation with Detroit residents without running water in their homes. We have paid $150,000 in water bills for 900 Detroiters so far.

I need help paying my bill

If your water was shut off due to an unpaid bill, we'll try to help you get your account paid off. Your privacy is guaranteed.

I want to help pay a bill

If you want to donate money to help pay off a water bill, we'll match you with someone in need. Give us some basic information to get started.

More than 300,000 Detroit residents face water shutoffs because of past due water bills.

You can help get their water turned back on.

How it works:

  • 1

    Enter your pledge in the form and we'll find a bill for you to pay.

  • 2

    We'll walk you through the steps to pay their bill with the Detroit Water & Sewage Department.

  • 3

    Congrats! You helped someone turn their water on. Tell the world.
